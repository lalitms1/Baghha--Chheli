GOAT & TIGER

How to Play

🐯 Tigers (2 Players): Start at corners. Move 1 step or Jump over a Goat to eat it. Win by eating 5 Goats.

🐐 Goats (1 Player): Place 20 goats one by one. Once all placed, move 1 step. Win by trapping all Tigers.

Lines: You can only move along the drawn lines.
